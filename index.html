How it works

Use your calculator in 3 simple steps

01

Make use of our calculator

Visit our calculator by clicking the calculator icon visible on the web page.It will open up our window for your calculator.

02

Give information as asked by calculator

Our calculator will ask you certain questions based on the parameters set by us. Please answer the questions appropriately on basis on which the calculator will calculate your carbon footprint

03

Check your carbon footprint !!

The calculator will then display your carbon footprint. It will also give you some suggestions on basis of your footprint. This will give you an idea about some things you can do to protect your enviroment
